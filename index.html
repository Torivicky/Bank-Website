Exclusive range of services to manage Your finance and spendings.

Securely you can connect all your bank account and track your spending from a single dashboard, giving you a holistic overview of your expenditure.

Savings

Unlimited Bank offers savings plans in local currencies; customers will get up to a 20% interest rate per annum on their savings, depending on the savings plan they sign up for.

Online Banking

Unlimited Bank modern web and mobile applications allow you to keep track of your finances wherever you are in the world

Loans

Customers of Unlimited Bank will request short-term loans from their app or dashboard. Loans will be approved based on the credit score of the customer.

Transfers

Unlimited Bank allows you to transfer money from your balance to other Unlimited users for free and other bank accounts; Get up to 50 free transfers monthly when you send money to other banks.

Physical & Virtual Cards

Never run out of money; the more you transact, the more you can spend even with lower balances. Customers will have the option of creating virtual naira and dollar cards for their online payment with Unlimited Bank.

Corporate Banking

Unlimited Bank provides business accounts and other ancillary services to SMEs. As a small business, managing inflows and outflows can be better done from your internet banking.
